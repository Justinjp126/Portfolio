National History Day

In 8th grade, I created my first website for National History Day (NHD). Using Weebly, I built the website, and it would place 2nd at a regional conference. This project was my first experience making websites, and it inspired me to join Technology Student Association when I was in high school.

2018

TSA Webmaster #1

In my first year in high school, I joined the Technology Student Association (TSA). My group and I competed in the Webmaster event, and we created a website about Wiz Khalifa using Weebly. We advanced to the state conference with this project. While building the website, I learned about design fundamentals, such as color, typography, and white space.

2020

Nursing Home Mask Initiative

My friend and his partner started the Nursing Home Mask Initiative at the beginning of the pandemic. They wanted to make an initiative to help nursing homes, who needed masks, get connected with mask-maskers. However, they needed a website to start their project, and that’s when my friend asked me to help. My friend and I created this website using multiple languages including, HTML, (S)CSS, and Javascript for the frontend, and Laravel and MySQL for the backend. This project took hundreds of hours to complete, over two months. All in all, we were able to get around 150 masks to nursing homes. We were also able to collaborate with the Pittsburgh Penguins to promote our initiative.
