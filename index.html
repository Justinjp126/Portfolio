Nursing Home Mask Initiative

My friend and his partner started the Nursing Home Mask Initiative at the beginning of the pandemic. They wanted to make an initiative to help nursing homes, who needed masks, get connected with mask-maskers. However, they needed a website to start their project, and thatâ€™s when my friend asked me to help. My friend and I created this website using multiple languages including, HTML, (S)CSS, and Javascript for the frontend, and Laravel and MySQL for the backend.
